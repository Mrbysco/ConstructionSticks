* Change the crafting recipes for the Construction Sticks
* A `Stick Template` has been added which is to be used in crafting the construction sticks in the Smithing Table
* The `Stick Template` can be crafted with 4 sticks surrounding 1 wooden button in the center
* The smithing recipe is 1 `Stick Template`, 1 `Stick` and the ingot/gem of the specific construction stick (For example a Diamond for a Diamond Stick)
